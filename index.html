History and Evolution of Cebu Technological University - Danao Campus

Cebu Technological University (CTU) Danao Campus is a dynamic educational institution located in Danao City, Cebu. As part of the CTU system, the campus offers a diverse range of programs in technology, engineering, education, and hospitality, with a strong focus on hands-on learning and innovation.

Early Beginnings

CTU Danao Campus traces its origins to a vocational school established to meet the growing educational needs of Danao City and its neighboring municipalities. It played a vital role in providing technical and vocational training to students in the region, contributing to the local workforce and industrial development.

Transition to a University

In 2009, Cebu State College of Science and Technology (CSCST), of which the Danao campus was a part, was converted into Cebu Technological University (CTU) by virtue of Republic Act 9744. This conversion marked the beginning of CTU's journey as a polytechnic university, offering a wide range of academic programs including undergraduate and graduate degrees across its campuses, including Danao.

Growth and Expansion

As a constituent campus of CTU, the Danao Campus expanded its academic offerings, infrastructure, and student services. Over the years, it has introduced degree programs in fields such as Engineering, Education, and Information Technology, catering to the growing demand for higher education in the northern Cebu region. The campus continues to contribute to local development through research, extension services, and partnerships with industries. It serves as a hub for academic excellence and innovation, promoting the university's mission to provide quality education to all. Today, CTU Danao Campus stands as a vital institution in the region, contributing to the educational, social, and economic progress of its community. This history showcases CTU Danao's commitment to providing quality education and its ongoing efforts to meet the needs of its students and community.

Empowering Futures: Inside the Vibrant Community of CTU Danao Campus

Cebu Technological University (CTU) Danao Campus is a thriving educational institution located in Danao City, Cebu. As part of the renowned CTU system, the campus offers a diverse array of programs in technology, engineering, education, and hospitality. Known for its emphasis on hands-on learning, innovation, and industry-aligned education, CTU Danao Campus empowers students to excel in their chosen fields. With state-of-the-art facilities, a dedicated faculty, and a strong focus on community development, the campus serves as a catalyst for personal and professional growth, nurturing future leaders and innovators.

Quadrangle

The CTU Danao Campus Quadrangle is a central open space that serves as a versatile hub for various campus activities.

SAC

The SAC is an indoor hall used for sports, assemblies, and other student events.

Library

The library supports academic and research needs with a wide collection of books, journals, and digital resources.

Bistro/Canteen

The canteen is a vibrant space where students, faculty, and staff can enjoy meals and snacks.

Grandstand

The Grandstand is a venue for large-scale events, from university assemblies to graduation ceremonies.

Centrum

The Fabrication Laboratory (FABLAB) at CTU Danao Campus is an advanced, open-access workshop equipped with modern tools and technologies designed to support innovation, creativity, and hands-on learning.

Tennis Court

The tennis court is used for physical education classes, sports training, and recreational activities.

Fitness Gym

The Fitness Gym at CTU Danao Campus promotes health and wellness among students, faculty, and staff.

Gender and Development

The Gender and Development (GAD) Center focuses on promoting gender equality and empowerment in the university community.
